Education for nursery and primary school children

Christ the King Nursery and Primary is located in Iceme Sub-County, Oyam District in northern Uganda. The school was established in 2018. The vision of the school is to produce pupils capable of meeting the demand for tomorrow. The pioneering enrollment for the school was 150 pupils. Today, the pupil population has increased to more than 600.
